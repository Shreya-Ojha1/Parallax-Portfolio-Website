About


Hi, I'm Shreya Ojha! a creative and curious soul with a passion for blending technology and design. I'm a UI/UX designer, graphic designer, frontend developer, and a growing data science enthusiast.My journey is driven by an endless fascination with how things work and how they can be made better. Beyond the tech world, I’m a travel enthusiast who finds inspiration in exploring new places and meeting new people. I believe that every interaction and destination adds a unique color to my perspective something I carry into every design and project I create. When I’m not coding or designing, you’ll find me creating content, capturing meaningful moments, and constantly seeking out new ways to express ideas visually and verbally. Content creation not only fuels my creativity but also sharpens my communication skills. In short I'm all about innovation, storytelling, and connection, whether it's through a user interface, a piece of art, or a travel story.
